Los Angeles

The Dodgers moved from Brooklyn in 1957 to LA and play at Dodger Stadium. Since they have came to LA they have had some of the biggest names in baseball on there team. Some examples are Clayton Kershaw, Duke Snider, Tommy Lasorda, and many other names. One of there most famous members of the team is the sports caster Vin Skully who has been with the Dodgers since Brooklyn in 1950. Vin Skully is set to retire this year at the age of 88. While the Dodgers have been in LA they have won six World Series. They have also appeared in 9 World Series.
