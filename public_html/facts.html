FUN FACTS

The Dodgers Franchise has had a interesting things about them that not a lot of people know about.
Fun Fact#1- The Stadium in LA was built on top of a ravine where there were homes with people living in these homes. The Police and other forms of goverment workers had to come in and get the people out of their homes and if you look it up online you can see photos of people being dragged out of their homes.
Fun Fact#2- The San Fransisco Giants and The Dodgers came to California at the same time and there rivalry was actually carried over from cities and now that one team is in San Fansisco and the other is in LA their rivalry got worse because of the cities own rivalry between economy, politics, and now sports.
Fun Fact#3- Even after the head coach of the team left Don Mattingly still manages the team and goes to most games.
Fun Fact#4- The Dodgers now have one of the best pitches in MLB history. His name is Clayton Kershaw is in the top ten list of most strikeouts with his Nasty Curveball.
Fun Fact#5- When the Dodgers moved to LA they did not yet have there own field to play on. In fact they did not have a field for about 3 years. But in 1961 Dodger Stadium opened and it is now the third oldest baseball stadium that is still operational.
